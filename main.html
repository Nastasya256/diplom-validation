Food delivery for 15 minutes or faster

Super Store —
is a new format of a store at home. The main difference from the usual ones is that you don't have to go to the store. Moreover, a trip to the nearest grocery store will most likely take you longer than a delivery from a Super Store.

Available in the Super. Food and Super. Taxi appications

How it works?

We are opening a store in your area. It works from 7 am to midnight.

In the store, instead of customers, there are storekeepers who collect your orders. One order is collected in a couple of minutes.

The collected order is delivered by courier. This takes 5-10 minutes. Delivery is free.

What do we sell?

What you can find in the grocery store: vegetables, fruits, dairy, bread, ready meals and further down the list.

We also have everything you might need at home — for you, for your animals, your equipment, and your children. And if it rains and you don't have an umbrella, we'll save you too — you won't even be late for your meeting.

Where does this work?

Look at the map. Blue indicates areas where the Store is already operating. Gray is the area where we are about to open. If your area isn't painted over, don't worry: we're probably already looking for a place there.
